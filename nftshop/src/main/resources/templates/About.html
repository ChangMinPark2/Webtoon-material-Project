    ABOUT

    WMSS is a Webtoon Meterial Sales Site. This is a global market participant-type webtoon material trading platform in the field of webtoons. Through the exchange of webtoon materials, we provide a more suitable working environment for existing webtoon creators. We support the continuous creative activities of artists who are experiencing financial difficulties.
